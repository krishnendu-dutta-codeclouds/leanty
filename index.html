Everything

Real answers about GLP-1 therapy, how Leanty works, and what to expect from your first dose onward.

What is GLP-1 therapy and how does it work?
GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a naturally occurring hormone that regulates appetite and blood sugar. GLP-1 medications mimic this hormone, reducing hunger signals, slowing digestion, and helping you feel full longer — leading to consistent, sustainable weight loss without extreme dieting.
How quickly will I see results?
Most patients notice reduced appetite within the first week. Visible weight loss typically begins in weeks 2–4, with clinically significant results (8–15% body weight) by month 6. Your physician titrates your dose to balance results with tolerability.
Do I need to visit a doctor in person?
No. Leanty is 100% online. You complete a 5-minute health intake, a board-certified physician reviews your case the same day, and your medication ships overnight — all without an in-person visit.
Is compounded semaglutide safe?
Yes. Our pharmacy is USP 797-compliant and follows strict sterile-compounding protocols. Every batch is third-party tested for potency, purity, and sterility. Your physician monitors you throughout treatment.
What does my subscription include?
Your monthly plan includes the physician consultation, ongoing dose adjustments, the medication, all injection supplies, cold-chain shipping, and 24/7 access to your care team through the patient portal. No hidden fees.
How is Leanty different from Ozempic or Wegovy?
We use the same active molecule (semaglutide) at clinical doses, but with three key differences: no insurance gatekeeping, personalized physician titration, and direct-to-door delivery with ongoing care — all at a flat, transparent monthly price.
